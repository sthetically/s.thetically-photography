How do I receive my pictures?

Your pictures are sent to you through a link via text or email (whichever you prefer) when editing is complete. You will be brought to a Google Drive folder where you can download any and all pictures to your phone or computer.

When do you receive my pictures?

I try to edit pictures as soon as possible so you should get your fully edited photos within a week or two of your photo session.

Should I give you a "shot list?"

You can send me an idea board that shows your vision for the desired pictures, but it is not required as you will describe your vision in words when filling out the booking form.

How do you edit the pictures?

I use Adobe lightroom classic and Adobe photoshop for my edits. I do not manipulate pictures by adding new things that were not once in the picture. However, I am willing to TRY to remove some objects from the photo, but this is not always an option.

Do I have to share your pictures with your logo?

You do not have to have my logo present when sharing the pictures I have taken. With this being said, however, I would very much appreciate if you tag me or promote the s.thetically name in some way in your post.

How can I download your pictures?

When on the desired picture on the Google Drive, click on the three dots in the upper right hand corner and then click on download and the picture will be saved to your photos.
