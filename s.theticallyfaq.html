How do I receive my pictures?

Your pictures are sent to you through a link via text and/or email (whichever is preferred) when editing is complete. You will be brought to a Google Drive folder where you can download any and all pictures to your personal phone or computer. If you do not have a google account to access google drive, we can discuss an alternative way of getting the pictures from my end to yours.

Should I prepare a "shot list?"

You can send me an idea board that shows your vision for the desired pictures, but it is not required as you will describe your vision in words when booking the session.

How do you edit the pictures?

I use Adobe Photoshot and Adobe Lightroom Classic for my edits. I do not manipulate pictures by adding new things that were not once in the picture. However, I am willing to TRY to remove some objects from the photo, but this is not always an option.

Do I have to share your pictures with your logo?

You do not have to have my logo present when sharing the picture I have taken. With this being said, however, it would be very much appreicated ifi you tag me or promote the s.thetically name in some way in your post.

How can I download your pictures?

When on the desired picture on the Google Drive, click on the three dots in the upper right hand corner and then click on download and the picture will be saved to your photos. You can also select all the pictures and download all to your device gallery at one time.

Do you print physical copies of the pictures?

I do not print the pictures, but if you do want them printed you can express that to me and I will be willing to get those for you, but keep in mind that it will cost extra for each print.
